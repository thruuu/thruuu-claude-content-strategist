# activecampaign.com

## Business
- **What they do**: Email marketing and marketing automation platform focused on customer experience automation. Helps businesses automate customer communications via email, SMS, and site messaging.
- **Core products/features**: Email marketing, marketing automation workflows, CRM, SMS marketing, landing pages, site tracking, lead scoring, segmentation.
- **Target audience**: Small to mid-size businesses, marketing teams, and agencies managing client email and automation.
- **Content goals**: Product signups, free trial conversions, brand authority in the marketing automation space.
- **Key competitors**: Mailchimp, HubSpot, Klaviyo, Brevo, GetResponse, Drip.
